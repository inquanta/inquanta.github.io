Inquanta helps communities track measurable contributions like words written, tasks completed, or hours studied ‚Äî all through Discord.

How does it work?

  • Inquanta features a simple, clean slash-command interface for its users.
  • Server admins can create custom counters for any measurable activity with custom units. For example, you can create a counter for "words" or "hours" to track writing or studying progress.
  • Users can contribute to these counters by adding their progress, which updates their contribution totals.
  • The bot maintains a leaderboard for each counter, allowing users to see how they rank against others in the community, fostering a sense of competition and motivation.
  • At the same time, we understand that not all users act in good faith. Inquanta includes features to ban users from contributing to specific counters, ensuring that the integrity of your community is maintained.
